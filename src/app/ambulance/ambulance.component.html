Types of Ambulance Available

Basic Ambulance

"Basic Life Support Ambulance" is for the patients who need medical transportation. It Comparies of patients bed, pulse oximetry and oxygen delivery device.

Advance Ambulance

"ALS Ambulance" is equipped with ventilator, ECG, Monitoring devices and paramedic staff.

Mortuary Ambulance

"Mortuary Ambulance" services are used for the transportation of the dead body.It is also called as Hearse van.

Neonatal Ambulance

"Neonatal Ambulance" services are a vital part of care for premature and sick babies, to ensure that babies can moved quickly for the right care in the right place for their needs.
